Symmetric Effects - Architecture

Clone effects whose params are DESIGNED to be automated - "a radial copy intended for you to automate radius on." Not a new mechanism: a design standard for effect params (motion params, continuous, zero-safe) applied to one new effect and two upgraded ones.

3 · Decisions & non-goals

DecisionWhy
Sequenced AFTER effect-param automationWithout lanes on effect settings these are just params with good taste; with lanes they're the demo. Hard dependency, deliberate.
New plugin, not a radialSymmetry rewriteExisting projects keep their look; Radial Copy ships with the standard baked in instead of bending an old effect's semantics.
The standard goes in the effect-authoring notesContinuous · zero-safe · phase-complete becomes three lines in effects/types.ts's doc comment - every future effect reads it.
Clone specs stay pure dataThese are CloneSpec-producing plugins like their siblings - per-frame values arrive via the merged-settings overlay; no new render machinery.
Non-goalsShader-side symmetric effects (kaleidoscope already exists) · per-clone note-reactivity (that's modulator territory) · 3D lattice cloning.
The one-sentence mental model: once effect settings take lanes, "designed for automation" is just three adjectives - continuous, zero-safe, phase-complete - applied to one new orbiting-copies effect and enforced backward on the two clones that almost qualify.
